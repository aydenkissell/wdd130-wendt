Welcome to Legend of the Six

Looking for a new job, Kasai Yama comes across a sleepy job in a normal looking bookstore. Well, he thought it was normal. Through this new job, Kasai and his friends discover just how fairytale-like the owner is and the secret he keeps is just the beginning.

Meet the Characters

Kasai Yama

Born into a rich family, 19 year old Kasai broke away from his Japanese heritage to live in America with his Korean grandmother. Even though he would rather be carefree and spend time helping his grandmother, Kasai is forced to be the adult in his friend group. Especially when their powers are put to the test against something they have never faced before.

Gabriel Smith

This fun-loving guy is all about the spotlight! Being 17, Gabriel disregards any sense of responsibility when it comes to magic.

Renee Malker

At 16 years old, this smart cookie is already ahead of her academic and magical career. Tender-hearted and thoughtful.

Lucas King

A military brat at 18 years old, Lucas is the complete opposite of his battle hardened father. He gets quite nervous.
