A New Fantasy Adventure Begins

Welcome

Looking for a new job, Kasai Yama stumbles into a sleepy little bookstore… except nothing about it is normal. What follows kicks off a supernatural adventure with six teens discovering powers, portals, and a very mysterious shopkeeper.

Main Characters

Kasai Yama

Responsible, level-headed, and way too stressed for a 19-year-old.

Gabriel Smith

Chaos incarnate. But lovable. Usually.

Renee Malker

Smart, steady, and way more mature than the rest of the group.

Lucas King

Anxious but loyal, with powers that freak him out.

Anauel Smith

Chill, grounded, and the group’s voice of reason.

Ryan Malker

Sweet 12-year-old with fire magic he definitely didn’t ask for.
