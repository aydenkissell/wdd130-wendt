Meet the Author

The imagination behind the magic.

Hello, I'm the Author! Kaitlyn Holloway!

Born into a creative family, I have always been exposed to artistic talent including drawing, painting, crocheting, writing, scrapbooking, etc… I started out following in my sister’s footsteps with sketching and coloring but quickly found my own niche in creative writing.

My first book, The Crescent Island, was a wonderful start and motivator in continuing my passion for exploring my imagination and sharing it with the world. My other projects, including Legend of the Six, are personal projects that have taken years in the making. I hope any traveler who comes across this page enjoys the adventures within!
